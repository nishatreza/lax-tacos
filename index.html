CUSTOMER REVIEWS

What they say:

Great food, great prices, and pretty quick service. I whole-heartedly recommend the California burrito with 'Al Pastor' meat (marinated pork). I usually call them beforehand and then within 10-15 minutes my order is ready for pick up. The chimichangas are also very delicious, and the carne asada fries are to die for! Give those a try if you're tired of always ordering tacos.

Dessire N. - Inglewood, CA

Came here cus was looking for carne asada nachos. I should have figured by the first comment I read "meat was cold". Yes meat was cold, which means cheese won't melt!! I usually like keeping the plastic bag closed so the heat can melt the cheese but not even that helped. I do have to give it to them the meat is seasoned well BUT not the best outcome. Maybe next time

Luis D. - Harbor Gateway, Los Angeles, CA

Love this place! I come here with my coworkers on fridays to get away from the office and enjoy some sunshine. I typically get three tacos but decided to go with the burrito today. I had the al pastor in the burrito and although delicious, it ended up being too much meat and a bit overwhelming so probably going to just stick with the tacos! They also have the best veggies on the side. Typically carrots, a little spicy.

Jennie C. - San Jose, CA
